About Me



Hello there, my name is Bill Windsor. My primary background is in science, engineering, and product planning in the electronics industry. I get extremely charged up about defining technical products that meet a significant customer need, and then in leading the development and execution of completing those product designs and getting them successfully into customers' hands and providing customer solutions. I also love hands-on science lab experiments; and I have assembled a substantial amount of science lab equipment and have led hands-on science lab classes for middle school students for five years, on a volunteer basis.


I enrolled in the UC Berkeley Full Stack Web Development Course to further address software solutions in the data science industry.
