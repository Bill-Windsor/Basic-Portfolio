About Me



Hello, my name is Bill Windsor. My primary background is in science, engineering, and product planning in networking semiconductors and digital signal processing applications. I get charged up about defining technical products that meet a significant customer need, then in leading technical development and execution of completing those product designs, and leading successful deployment into the market, delivering customer solutons, thereby driving corporate revenue.



I graduated from MIT with a BSEE degree, and from Harvard Business School with an MBA degree. I love to conduct science lab experiments: I have assembled a substantial amount of science lab equipment and have led hands-on science lab classes for middle school students for five years, on a volunteer basis.



I enrolled in the UC Berkeley Full Stack Web Development Course to pursue a software development career, with special interest and expertise in the data science industry, utilizing my education and career experience in networking and digital signal processing applications.
